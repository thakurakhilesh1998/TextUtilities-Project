About

Welcome to the Text Utility Web Application – your go-to platform for effortless text manipulation. Whether you're a writer, coder, or student, our user-friendly interface allows you to perform various operations on your text with ease. Explore features such as finding and replacing text, reversing text order, changing case, counting words and characters, removing duplicates, sorting text, generating Lorem Ipsum, and encoding/decoding in Base64. Additionally, you can compress and decompress text, or simply clear your workspace. Our intuitive design ensures smooth navigation on desktops, tablets, and mobiles. Simply input your text, choose an operation, click the corresponding button, and view the instant results. Enhance your text editing experience with Text Utility – where manipulation meets simplicity. Start crafting your text effortlessly today!
